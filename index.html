Introduction

Strategy Without the Jargon

Five years into business strategy, I've learned that good frameworks beat fancy buzzwords. Every time.

I spent my first three years at Wipro working on autonomous vehicle projects, where I learned that complexity is inevitable but confusion is optional. Good documentation, clear communication, and structured problem-solving became second nature.

Then I moved to Susamridh Nidhi Bank, where I led strategic growth initiatives that expanded financial inclusion across underserved regions. Drove 48% customer acquisition growth in two quarters by building frameworks that executives actually used instead of filing away. Applied predictive analytics to enable 30% revenue growth. Made numbers tell compelling stories.

Along the way, I picked up Power BI, advanced Excel wizardry, Python automation, and an engineering degree from VIT with an 8.94 GPA. Got certified by Microsoft (twice), Google, and various platforms that prove I can walk the talk.

Currently pursuing my MBA at University of Edinburgh Business School, where I'm questioning everything I thought I knew about business while preparing for roles in strategy consulting and product management.

When I'm not dissecting case studies or optimizing processes, you'll find me trekking through hills (best ideas happen at altitude), exploring cities on foot (architecture tells better stories than spreadsheets), or journaling reflective thoughts (writing clarifies thinking).
